word: bonjour
translation: hello, good morning, good day
type: phrase
group: 👋 Greetings & phrases
pronunciation: /bɔ̃.ʒuʁ/
example.fr: Bonjour, comment allez-vous ?
example.en: Hello, how are you?
etymology: From 'bon' (good) + 'jour' (day).
note: Standard greeting used throughout the day until evening.
