word: hallo
translation: hello, hi
type: interjection
group: 👋 Greetings & phrases
pronunciation: /haˈloː/
example.de: Hallo! Wie geht's?
example.en: Hello! How's it going?
etymology: A relatively modern loan, likely from English "hallo/hello", itself from Old High German "halâ" (a call to attract attention).
note: Neutral and works with both du and Sie — one of the few German greetings that isn't register-sensitive.
