word: hola
translation: hello, hi
type: phrase
group: 👋 Greetings & phrases
pronunciation: /ˈola/
example.es: ¡Hola, ¿qué tal tu día?
example.en: Hello, how is your day?
etymology: Possibly from Old Spanish 'ola', or from 'hola' as an exclamation to attract attention.
note: Very common; the 'h' is silent.
