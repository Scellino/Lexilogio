word: hallo
translation: hello, hi
type: interjection
group: 👋 Greetings & phrases
pronunciation: /ˈɦɑ.loː/
example.nl: Hallo! Hoe gaat het met je?
example.en: Hello! How are you?
etymology: A shared Germanic greeting also found as German "hallo" and English "hello", possibly from an old call used to hail a ferryman or attract attention.
note: Neutral greeting suitable in most situations.
